AT THE ZOO RESTAURANT

Centrally located in the zoo and adjacent to the concert lawn, Africafe is a great stop for families before beginning their journey into Africa. Do some birdwatching while dining on a selection of quick favorites such as cheeseburgers, chicken strips, Zenner's hot dogs, fresh salads, wraps and shoestring fries.

A spot just for adults! Stop by the silver Beer & Wine cart in Elephant Plaza to grab an ice cold local craft beer, canned cocktail or glass of wine and enjoy from the concert lawn while taking in views of Elephant Lands.
